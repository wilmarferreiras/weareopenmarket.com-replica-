We Are Open Market

We are a socially responsible marketplace redistributing capital and
customers to alll BIPOC(Black Indigenous People Of Color) and POC
(People Of Color) businesses.

Our mission is to make it easy for consumers to locate and support BIPOC
(Black Indigenous People Of Color) brands that align with the initiatives
they care about -- social justice, equity, sustainability, and livable wages.

Our Driving Mission:Close.Create.Cultivate

Our mission empowers BIPOC and POC-owned
brands to flourish in an ever-expanding digital
world. A pivotal role in fostering the overall
economic growth and prosperity of our society.

Fashion and beauty to food and art, our events,
highlight BIPOC and POC-owned businesses,
offering customers an unparalleled opportunity
to discover and support unique brands.

The community serves as our foundation,
enabling us to cultivate connections, foster
mutual understanding, and contribute to a more
equitable and representative society.
